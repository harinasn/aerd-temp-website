About Us

AERD (formerly SGS Electronics & Enterprises) has been a trusted name in technology and services since 1992. Starting as a sole proprietorship, AERD has grown into a strong team of professionals and engineers, delivering turnkey projects for both government and private sectors. We specialize in network infrastructure, computer services, and furniture solutions, ensuring quality and excellence for all our clients.

  • Over 30 years of expertise in the industry.
  • Extensive experience in government projects and corporate collaborations.
  • Authorized distributors of major global technology brands.

Why Choose Us?

With decades of experience, certified professionals, and a client-first approach, AERD is the perfect partner for your technology and service needs. We offer comprehensive solutions tailored to the unique requirements of every client.

  • Unmatched expertise in network infrastructure and service integration.
  • Proven track record in delivering large-scale government projects.
  • End-to-end solutions, including furniture installations and IT services.
